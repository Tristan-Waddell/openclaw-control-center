# Qualification rubric (simple)

Score each lead 0-100.

## Criteria
- Role fit (0-35)
- ICP org fit (0-25)
- Contact quality (0-25)
- Intent/timing signal (0-15)

## Confidence bands
- 80-100: High
- 50-79: Medium
- 0-49: Low

## Notes
- If only generic contact exists, cap Contact quality at 10.
- If source is older/unclear, reduce Intent/timing signal.
