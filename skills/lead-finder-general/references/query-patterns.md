# Query patterns

Use these patterns and adapt placeholders.

## Core
- "<industry> association directory <state>"
- "<segment> administrators contact <state>"
- "site:<domain> staff directory"
- "site:<domain> contact us"
- "<organization type> commissioner email"

## Local/public-sector
- "<state> department <sector> directory"
- "<county/city> <sector> administration contact"

## B2B org discovery
- "top <industry> companies <region>"
- "<industry> member directory"
- "<industry> conference exhibitor list"

## Validation queries
- "<person name> <organization>"
- "site:<org domain> <person name>"
- "<role title> <org> email"
